# theme-exploration

## Purpose

Produce a structured philosophical exploration of the theme,
grounded in the structural question and evidence layer.

This is interpretive but must remain anchored to evidence.

## Input

theme: string
structural_question: string
evidence_layer: structured evidence output

## Output Format (strict)

Turn 1 – Conceptual Frame
<300+ words>

Turn 2 – Societal Reframing
<300+ words>

Turn 3 – Psychological Implications
<300+ words>

Turn 4 – Institutional Dynamics
<300+ words>

Turn 5 – Long-Term Trajectory
<300+ words>

## Constraints

- No divider markers
- No meta-commentary
- No user Q&A simulation
- No invented follow-up questions
- Anchor insights to evidence layer
- Maintain near-future framing (0–5 years).
